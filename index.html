About Me

I am a Cameroonian young male, currently pursuing the MSc in Advanced Computer Science at the University of Oxford. I previously obtained my MEng. in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics at Toulouse INP - ENSEEIHT, where I majored in High Performance Computing and Big Data.

My research interests lie in the fields of Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing, in which I want to pursue a PhD and build my career.

I'm currently a part-time researcher at the Oxford Artificial Intelligence Society, where I have the chance to be part of a team of passionate and talented young researchers, investigating biases in large-scale generative language models.

Additionally, I am a Co-Founder of Quidely and Skylon.
